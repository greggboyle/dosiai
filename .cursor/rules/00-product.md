# DOSI.AI Product Context

DOSI.AI ("Dossier") is a B2B SaaS market intelligence platform for small-to-mid-market companies. It sweeps multiple AI vendors (OpenAI, Anthropic, xAI) for intelligence about a workspace's competitors and market, scores each finding for relevance to that specific company (the Market Intelligence Score / MIS), and presents results as a continuously updated feed plus higher-order surfaces (competitor profiles, briefs, battle cards, win/loss tracking, customer voice, channels).

Core principle: the MIS measures how significant an event is to the user's company, not to the company the event is about.

Audience: small-to-mid-market companies. Three buyer archetypes: scrappy founder/PMM, dedicated PMM function, mid-market CI program. Pricing reflects this with four tiers (Trial / Starter / Team / Business) plus a future Enterprise.
